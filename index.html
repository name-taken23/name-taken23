SQL TASK

Tasked with writing a subquery against the database pictured.
I decided to find what movie has a rating greater than 5, and is also of the action genre.

SELECT *
FROM movie
WHERE movie.mov_id IN
(SELECT rating.mov_id FROM rating
WHERE rating.rev_stars > 5.00)
AND movie.mov_id IN
(SELECT movie_genres.mov_id FROM movie_genres
INNER JOIN genres
ON genres.gen_id = movie_genres.gen_id
WHERE genres.gen_title = 'Action');
